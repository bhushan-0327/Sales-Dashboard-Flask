Got Questions? We've Got Answers.

Q: Do I need technical knowledge to use DashVieww?

A: Not at all! If you can use basic apps like WhatsApp, you can easily use DashVieww. Our interface is designed to be incredibly user-friendly.

Q: What file types are supported for upload?

A: DashVieww supports standard Excel files (.xlsx) and Comma Separated Values (.csv) files. Most accounting and POS systems can export in these formats.

Q: Will DashVieww work on my mobile phone or tablet?

A: Absolutely! DashVieww is a web-based application, meaning you can access your dashboard from any device with an internet browser – be it a phone, tablet, or PC.

Q: What happens to my sales data after I upload it?

A: Your privacy and data security are paramount. Your uploaded files are solely used to generate your insights and are not stored, shared, or used for any other purpose by DashVieww. We do not retain your raw data.
